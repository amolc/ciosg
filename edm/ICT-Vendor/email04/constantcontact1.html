What is CIO CHOICE?

CIO CHOICE is a powerful B2B marketing & sales acceleration platform that helps ICT Vendors promote their Products, Services and Solutions directly to 700+ CIOs in Singapore

How does the Platform work?
  • CIOs across Singapore take part in our unbiased online survey
  • Our Independent Advisory Panel study and endorse the results of the survey to establish the CIO's preferred choice
  • The CIO CHOICE Red Carpet Night recognises and honours Vendors within their respective categories
  • Each category winner is presented with the title of CIO CHOICE
